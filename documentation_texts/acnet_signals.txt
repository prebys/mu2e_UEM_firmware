https://www-bd.fnal.gov/Elog/api/get/file/binary?fileId=421501
D:DRWCM, D:SXCOS, D:QX301, D:LIBS936A



D:DRWCM – Delivery Ring wall current monitor; measures circulating beam intensity in the Delivery Ring before resonant extraction begins.

D:SXCOS - Cosine component of sextupole magnet

D:QX301 – quadrupole (or its current/command readback) in the resonant extraction region of the Delivery Ring; tied to the extraction optics and timing when RX starts.

D:LI2Q3 – loss integrator in the 2Q extraction section of the Delivery Ring; integrated radiation/loss over the cycle near the RX region.

D:LI2Q4 – same as above, adjacent loss integrator in the 2Q extraction section.

D:LI2Q5 – same as above, third loss integrator in a row in the 2Q extraction section.

D:LQQ001 – fast beam loss monitor channel (UBLM fast sum) near a quadrupole in the Delivery Ring; instantaneous radiation / loss rate spike detector.

D:SA12VO – spectrum analyzer video output signal; analog RF/microstructure diagnostic level, not a beam intensity device.

D:DRLIFE – computed Delivery Ring beam lifetime in minutes based on decay of circulating beam intensity.

D:TORABT – toroid beam current transformer in the abort line; measures beam intensity sent to the abort/dump branch.

D:ICABT – ion chamber in the abort line; measures radiation and beam loss dose in the abort transport.

D:M4WCM – wall current monitor in the M4 beamline; measures extracted beam intensity after the beam leaves the Delivery Ring.

D:IC933 – ion chamber near the M4 diagnostic absorber region; measures radiation and beam loss close to the absorber.

D:LIBS936A – loss integrator at M4 lattice location ~936A near the diagnostic absorber; integrated total beam loss or accumulated dose over the spill.

D:LQCOL907B – fast beam loss monitor at a collimator in the M4/diagnostic region; instantaneous radiation/loss rate near the collimator.
